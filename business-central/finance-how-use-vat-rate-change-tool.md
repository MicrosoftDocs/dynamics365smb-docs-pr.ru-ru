### <a name="to-prepare-vat-rate-change-conversions"></a><span data-ttu-id="d43e4-120">Подготовка преобразований при изменении ставки НДС</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d43e4-120">To prepare VAT rate change conversions</span></span>
