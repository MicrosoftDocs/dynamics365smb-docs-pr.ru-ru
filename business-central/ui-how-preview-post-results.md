2. <span data-ttu-id="f32c7-107">Создайте счет покупки.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f32c7-107">Create a purchase invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f32c7-108">Дополнительные сведения см. в разделе [Регистрация покупок](purchasing-how-record-purchases.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f32c7-108">For more information, see [Record Purchases](purchasing-how-record-purchases.md).</span></span>
